Welcome to G-Force 19013

We are a competitive FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) robotics team from the Technology Innovation Hub. Our mission is to inspire young minds in STEM through robotics, coding, and engineering innovation.

Our team participates in regional and national FTC competitions, focusing on designing, building, and programming robots to complete specific challenges. We emphasize collaboration, problem-solving, and technical excellence.

About Our Team

G-Force 19013 was established in 2019 with the goal of making robotics accessible to all students in our community. Since our inception, we've grown from a small group of enthusiasts to a competitive team with multiple awards at regional tournaments.

Our team consists of 15 dedicated high school students with diverse interests in mechanical engineering, software development, electrical systems, and strategic planning. We mentor younger students in our community through outreach programs and workshops.

Each season, we design and build a new robot to compete in the annual FTC challenge. Our design process emphasizes iterative prototyping, testing, and refinement to create a reliable and high-performing robot.
